Getting there

By car

Parking at Osler House is free for our guests. If the car park is full you can use the John Radcliffe car park, but there will be a charge. See their page for car users.

To get to the car park, enter the John Radcliffe site from Osler Road, and follow the signs to Osler Lodge.

By bus

Any buses that go to the John Radcliffe Hospital will be a good choice as Osler House is in the grounds of the Hospital.

Get off the bus inside the John Radcliffe grounds and head up hill, keeping the Maternity unit/Women's centre to your left. Turn right outside the upper entrance to the Womem's centre and you should be able to find Osler House ahead and to the left.

Gifts

The best gift you can give to Eric is your love and support as he grows up, but if you would like to give him something to remember the day but are unsure what to get, we received a great of help from the breast-feeding service at the John Radcliffe hospital to turn Eric's weight loss around. Donations to this worthy cause would be fitting.
