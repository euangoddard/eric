Getting there

By car

There should be ample parking at the venue and if the car park is full, there is ample parking at the John Radcliffe hospital, round the corner.

Parking at Osler House is free for our guests. If the car park is full and you use the John Radcliffe car park, there will be a charge. See their page for car users.

Any buses that go to the John Radcliffe Hospital will be a good choice as Osler House is in the grounds of the Hospital.
